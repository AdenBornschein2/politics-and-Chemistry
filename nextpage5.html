    EQ: How do temperature,pressure, and volume relate to gas behavior?
    Molecules and gas have no volume and have no specific shape and there are no attractive or repulsive forces between gas molecules
    Pressure=the force exerted by a substance(gas) per unit area on another surface (walls of"container")
    = gasses flow from high to low pressure
